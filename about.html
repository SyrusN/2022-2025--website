Who is Syrus?

I am someone who likes to know how the inner processes and workings of everyday computing works. Nowadays this often includes things like machine learning, cryptography, computer networking, and more.

Besides learning about technology, I also like to think about how existing products--both physical and virtual--work via analysis of their basic mechanisms.
